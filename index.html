What is Hair ?

Hair is simple in structure, but has important functions in social functioning. Hair is made of a tough protein called keratin. A hair follicle anchors each hair into the skin.Also,hair is taken care of like the human body. We groom , style , wash and add on to our hair.

Types of Hair
What type do you have?

There are many different types of hair. There is curly , straight ,coiled and etc.Hair is most of the time genectic depending on your race/nationality.You can be in your natural state which means your hair whose texture hasn't been altered by chemical straighteners, including relaxers and texturizers.

Tips on Healthy Hair
Are you using the corect ingredients to create healthy hair?

Everyone's process is not the same.It does depends on what type of hair you have.But,there are ingredients that help all types of hair.For example, eggs-for protein,water,coconut oil and a trim that cuts the dead ends of your hair , so your dead ends won't prevent your hair growth.There are many more different techniques to increase hair growth.
